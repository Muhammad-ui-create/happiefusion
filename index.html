The Mushrooms

Three mushrooms.
Each doing one job.

No proprietary blends. Just the doses on the label — the doses studied in the research.

🧠

Lion's Mane

900mg per can

Hericium erinaceus — the nootropic mushroom. Studied for its ability to support Nerve Growth Factor (NGF) synthesis, which plays a role in the maintenance and repair of neurons. The research points to improved cognitive clarity, focus, and memory. The "I just remembered why I sat down" mushroom.

Key benefit: Cognitive clarity · Focus · Memory support

⚡

Cordyceps

700mg per can

Cordyceps militaris — the endurance mushroom. Used by athletes and studied for oxygen utilization and VO₂ max support. It helps your body produce ATP more efficiently, meaning real energy that doesn't spike and crash. The lift without the caffeine cliff.

Key benefit: Sustained energy · Stamina · O₂ utilization

🌿

Reishi

400mg per can

Ganoderma lucidum — the "queen of mushrooms." Adaptogenic, meaning it helps your body adapt to stress rather than just masking it. Studied for cortisol regulation, immune support, and improving sleep quality. Calm, on tap.

Key benefit: Stress adaptation · Calm · Sleep quality

Why fruiting body?

We use 100% fruiting body extract. Not mycelium.

Most mushroom supplements on the market use mycelium-on-grain — essentially the root structure of the mushroom grown on grain substrate. The problem? You're mostly getting grain starch, not active mushroom compounds. The beta-glucan content (the thing that actually does something) is a fraction of what you'd find in fruiting body extract.

Fungi Fusion uses fruiting body extract — the actual mushroom cap — standardized for beta-glucan content. Every dose on the label is the active compound dose, not the raw mushroom powder weight.

About the Mushrooms

What mushrooms are in Fungi Fusion?+
Every can contains 900mg Lion's Mane, 700mg Cordyceps, and 400mg Reishi — all as fruiting body extracts. That's 2,000mg total per can.
What's the difference between fruiting body and mycelium?+
Fruiting body is the actual mushroom — the part above ground with the cap. Mycelium is the root structure, and most cheap supplements use mycelium grown on grain, which means you're mostly getting grain starch. Fungi Fusion uses 100% fruiting body extract, standardized for beta-glucan content — the active compound.
Does Fungi Fusion contain caffeine?+
No. Fungi Fusion is completely caffeine-free. The energy and focus you feel comes from the mushrooms — Lion's Mane for cognitive clarity, Cordyceps for sustained energy through ATP synthesis — not from stimulants.
Are there any adaptogens besides mushrooms?+
No. We keep the formula focused — three mushrooms, each doing one job. No ashwagandha, no rhodiola, no ginseng. Just the stack.

Dosing & Usage

When should I drink Fungi Fusion?+
Any time you'd normally reach for coffee or an energy drink. Morning for focus, pre-workout for energy, afternoon slump for a lift without the sleep disruption, or evening wind-down (Reishi supports relaxation without sedation). There's no wrong time — it's caffeine-free so it won't keep you up.
How many cans per day?+
One to two cans per day is the recommended range. The 2,000mg dose per can already puts you in therapeutic territory — more isn't necessarily better, but two cans is safe and some people find it more effective for sustained support throughout the day.
How long does it take to feel the effects?+
Some effects (like the Cordyceps energy boost) can be felt within an hour of your first can. Lion's Mane and Reishi effects tend to be more cumulative — most customers report noticing a difference after 1–2 weeks of consistent daily use.

Ingredients & Sourcing

Where are your mushrooms sourced?+
Our mushroom extracts are sourced from certified organic farms and third-party tested for heavy metals, pesticides, and microbial contaminants. We source Lion's Mane and Reishi from China (the region with the longest history of cultivation and research), and Cordyceps from controlled indoor cultivation.
Are Fungi Fusion drinks vegan and gluten-free?+
Yes to both. Fungi Fusion is vegan, gluten-free, and free from the top 9 allergens. The only sweetener is a small amount of erythritol (a naturally-occurring sugar alcohol with zero glycemic impact).
Are there lab tests I can see?+
Yes. Certificate of Analysis (COA) documents are available on request. Email hello@happiefusion.com and we'll send the current batch test results.
